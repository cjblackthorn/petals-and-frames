About

Quiet storytelling through light, atmosphere, and emotion.

Petal & Frame Photography is rooted in softness, transitional light, weather, reflection, and moments that feel emotionally lived-in instead of overly staged.

The work focuses on natural movement, calm environments, and creating images that feel timeless instead of trend-driven. Much of the visual language comes from the Pacific Northwest: mist, coastlines, rain, layered skies, flowers, forests, and evenings that linger softly.

Rather than chasing perfection, Petal & Frame leans into atmosphere, texture, softness, and emotional honesty. The goal is not simply to document how something looked, but to preserve how it felt to exist inside that moment.

The portfolio includes portraits, florals, landscapes, atmospheric imagery, fine art photography, and quiet editorial storytelling.
