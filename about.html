About

Quiet storytelling through light, atmosphere, and emotion.

Petal & Frame Photography is rooted in softness, transitional light, weather, reflection, and moments that feel emotionally lived-in instead of overly staged.

Visual Direction

Atmosphere First

The work focuses on natural movement, calm environments, and images that feel timeless instead of trend-driven. Mist, coastlines, rain, layered skies, flowers, forests, and quiet evenings heavily influence the visual language.

Creative Approach

Gentle Collaboration

Sessions are intended to feel relaxed, emotionally safe, and collaborative. Soft prompting and natural interaction matter more than rigid posing or forced performance.

Portfolio Focus

Emotion & Memory

The goal is not simply to preserve how something looked, but how it felt to exist inside that moment. Portraits, landscapes, florals, editorial storytelling, and atmospheric imagery all connect back to that idea.
