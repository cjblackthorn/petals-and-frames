Frequently Asked Questions

Do you help with posing?

Yes. Sessions use gentle prompts and natural movement instead of rigid posing.

How long until galleries are delivered?

Most galleries are delivered within 2–3 weeks depending on workload, editing needs, and session size.

Do you offer rush delivery?

Limited rush delivery may occasionally be available depending on schedule and project scope. Significant additional fees apply and not all requests can be accommodated.

Do you photograph weddings?

Petal & Frame does not currently offer wedding-day photography coverage. Couples and engagement-style sessions are welcome.
